Common Questions

How is this different from a standard deep clean? +

A standard deep clean handles general surfaces more thoroughly than a maintenance clean. The Home Reset goes significantly further — inside appliances, behind and under furniture and appliances, grout scrubbing, high dusting, cabinet interiors, and detail areas most services skip entirely. It's more labor, more time, more skill, and a dramatically better result.

How long does the Home Reset take? +

Depending on the size and condition of your home, the Reset typically takes between 4 and 8 hours. We plan accordingly so nothing is rushed. You'll know the expected time window before we arrive.

Do you clean behind and underneath appliances? +

Yes — for all reachable appliances. This includes the refrigerator, stove, and other major kitchen appliances. We move them (safely), clean underneath and behind, then put them back. This is one of the signature zones of the Home Reset.

Do you bring your own supplies and products? +

Yes, completely. We arrive with everything needed — professional-grade products, tools, and equipment specific to each task. You don't need to provide or prepare anything. Just be ready to enjoy the result.

Can I set up recurring maintenance after the Reset? +

Absolutely — and we encourage it. Once your home has been reset to a clean baseline, ongoing maintenance cleans are much faster and more affordable. Many of our Home Reset clients become long-term monthly or bi-weekly clients. We'll discuss options when we deliver your quote.

Is this service good before guests, holidays, or moving? +

It's perfect for any of those situations. The Home Reset was specifically designed for moments when you need your home at its absolute best — not just tidy, but genuinely impressive. We recommend booking as early as possible, as calendar availability around peak times fills quickly.

What does pricing look like? +

Pricing is custom-quoted based on your home's size, number of rooms, current condition, and specific scope. We don't use a one-size-fits-all price because no two homes are the same. What we can promise is that the value far exceeds the cost — and there are no hidden fees. Request your quote and we'll have a number to you within 24 hours.

Is this right for a home that's in rough shape? +

The Home Reset was built exactly for homes in that situation. The more buildup, the more this service shines compared to a standard clean. We assess the condition during our quote process to ensure we scope and staff appropriately. We've seen it all — and we're not intimidated.
